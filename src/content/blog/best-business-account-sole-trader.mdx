Running a JDG (jednoosobowa działalność gospodarcza) or a small company? The
right business account keeps your fees low and your bookkeeping simple. Here's
what to look for.

## What matters for a business account

Unlike personal accounts, business accounts are judged on a different set of
features:

- Free or low monthly maintenance
- Free transfers to ZUS and the tax office (US)
- Integrations with accounting software
- Multi-user access for your accountant

### Watch out for cash-handling fees

If your business takes cash, check deposit and withdrawal fees carefully — they
can add up fast.

## Our top pick for sole traders

## Opening an account

1. Have your NIP and business registration ready.
2. Apply online and verify your identity.
3. Connect your accounting tool and invite your accountant.

## The bottom line

Don't overpay for a business account. Compare maintenance fees, transfer costs
and integrations side by side, and pick the one that matches how you actually
run your business.
